Assume that BITS runs on bio gas electricity and due to an accident, the bio gas resources have abruptly been depleted and the campus runs out of power. And since the campus is full of energy and is loaded with a technological extravaganza that is Apogee, you need power and you have run out of resources. The fest begins in half an hour. Now, you need appropriate reactants and reagents to produce biogas but you are not sure where to find them on campus. Luckily, your chemistry professor has left you a huge mind blowing clue base to find these chemicals and their locations. Now, using your knowledge of basic chemistry and even more importantly, your knowledge of BITS as your campus try to figure out where and what these chemicals are in your campus and find out how you will produce this biogas and supply it to your power plant at BITS.
